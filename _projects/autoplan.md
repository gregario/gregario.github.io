Founded AutoPlan in 2020 to help architects deal with the nightmare of planning applications. We were backed by NDRC (a top 5 European accelerator), ESA BIC Ireland, Enterprise Ireland, and joined the Nvidia Inception programme.

I built the AI and, because someone had to understand the law, got myself a diploma in planning law from King's Inns. We partnered with Google, Amazon, and AutoDesk along the way. The core idea was simple: planning rules are complex but deterministic, which makes them a perfect fit for AI to parse and apply.
